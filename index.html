About Me

Hello my name is Bryan Shay and I am a 31 year old Marketing Coordinator from Cleveland, Ohio. Before the shut down, I worked in Downtown Cleveland and had been for almost a decade. The best part about working downtown is all the tasy food you can eat for lunch. I signed up for the CWRU Coding Bootcamp to increase my job competency as well as open new avenues for my career. I have always been fascinated by technology and I don't see any end in site for the evolution of what we will be capable of doing. I am very interested in seeing where VR and AR lead us.


I have lots of hobbies that I like to do for fun. Painting is my current favorite, followed closely by gardening. This summer I grew tomatoes, basil, eight different types of peppers, and pickling cucumbers. I am also a novice blade smith, my roomate and I built a propane forge and have been learning the art! It's an amazing amount of fun.
